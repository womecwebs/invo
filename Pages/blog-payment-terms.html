Net 7 vs Net 15 vs Net 30: Which Payment Terms Should You Use?

Payment terms tell clients when an invoice is due. The right term protects your cash flow without surprising the client.

Direct Answer

Use Net 7 for fast-moving freelance work, Net 15 for most small service projects, and Net 30 for larger companies with slower approval processes.

Comparison Table

Term Best For Cash Flow Impact
Net 7 Freelancers, deposits, small tasks Fastest
Net 15 Agencies, consultants, local services Balanced
Net 30 Corporate clients, procurement teams Slowest

Pros and Cons

Short terms improve cash flow but may feel strict to large clients. Long terms feel familiar to corporate buyers but can strain small businesses.

Example

A consultant billing a startup might use Net 15, while a contractor requiring materials upfront might request a 50% deposit and Net 7 on the remaining balance.
