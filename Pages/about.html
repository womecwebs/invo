About

INVO exists to make business documents calm, fast and trustworthy.

We believe small businesses should not need heavy accounting software just to send a polished invoice, quote or receipt.

Mission

Help independent professionals create clear business documents in minutes.

Vision

Become a complete business toolkit with AI assistance, payments, templates and customer workflows.

Values

Simplicity, trust, speed, accessibility, privacy and practical business outcomes.

Timeline

2026: static toolkit foundation. Next: accounts, recurring invoices, payments and premium templates.

Why INVO Exists

Many freelancers lose time formatting documents instead of serving clients. INVO removes that friction.

Meet the Product

A fast, handcrafted HTML, CSS and JavaScript application designed to grow into a full SaaS platform.
