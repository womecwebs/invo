Privacy Policy

INVO is designed privacy-first. In this static version, document drafts are saved in your browser local storage and are not sent to a server by the app.

Information you enter

Invoice, quote and receipt data remains in the browser unless a future cloud account, payment integration or backend API is connected.

Local storage

Draft saving helps returning users continue work. You can reset drafts from each builder page.

Future integrations

Payments, analytics, AI features and accounts will require clear notices before activation.
